A read-along audiobook for Ancient Greek, whit real quotes from Homer to Plutarch

(produced by Amazing Recording Studios)

This project is intended as a collection of quotes from classic Ancient Greek authors that you can hear spoken in the original Ancient Greek.

There is a shortage of resources for beginners of Greek, in particular of audio resources to hear it spoken, for those that study it by themselves. This project aims to fill that void, by creating an open platform in which everyone is encouraged to contribute by recording themselves while reading Greek and sending us the audio files to include them in the website, app and ebook.

The idea is also that starting Greek from real quotes (like epic and poetic ones from the Iliad and the Odyssey) is more engaging and meaninful than the usual plain and made up phrases used in typical grammar textbooks.
