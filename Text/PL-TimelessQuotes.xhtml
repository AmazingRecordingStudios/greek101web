Timeless quotes

ἥδομαι φίλοις ἀγαθοῖς

(Socrates in Xenophon's Memorabilia 1.6.14)

("my delight is to be with good friends")

ἥδομαι: "I take delight in" + DAT, verb 1st sg pres ind mp

φίλοις: "friends", DAT pl masc (NOM sg: φίλος)

ἀγαθοῖς: "good" adj DAT pl masc (NOM sg m: ἀγαθός)
